# Mini Course: How I Systemize My Life With AI

*A better way to use AI*

This course was previously on another platform, you may have already seen it. I’m transferring it here to have everything on Substack.

By the end of this course, you should have the knowledge at your disposal to create almost anything you want with AI.

While I can't go over every single example on earth, I recommend that you *try to apply what you learn to the tasks you want to use AI for.*

Think about what you do every day and how AI could speed up *or *enhance that process.

For everything you do during the day, ask yourself, could I make this better with AI?
