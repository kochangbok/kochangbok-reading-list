# Prompt: Generate 50+ (Good) Content Ideas

*never run out of ideas again*

I’m not a fan of AI writing for me.

However, I am a fan of overwhelming my brain with good ideas so that my subconscious can come up with great ideas.

The thing about social media, or content in general, is that there are “types” of ideas that tend to always perform well due to human psychology.

Sure, you can (and should) publish ideas that randomly come to you, but this can be useful if you are feeling stuck and need a starting point.

This prompt pulls in information about your topic and target audience to spit out ideas based on:

- Audience pain points

- Audience desires

- Personal stories

- Harsh truths

- Novel perspectives

- Beginner advice

- Listicle ideas

- Potential hooks
