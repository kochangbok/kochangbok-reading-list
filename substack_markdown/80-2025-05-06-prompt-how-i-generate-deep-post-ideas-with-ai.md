# Prompt: How I Generate Deep Post Ideas With AI

*Because AI still sucks at writing the posts (and that's not fun anyway)*

I’ve been trying to get AI to write *good *social posts for the longest time.

I’ve gotten pretty damn close (you can go to the Write Viral Social Posts option in Kortex chat), but they still don’t feel right.

I’ve fed AI extremely detailed instructions with the perfect examples and still, it’s good for giving me ideas in the form of posts but it’s pretty damn difficult to get it right 100% of the time.

And… I just can’t do it.

I don’t want AI to write for me.

So for the past 6 months or so I’ve experimented with different ways of generating post ideas that fit *my needs.*

And I’m picky.

After all of that, I think I have something worth sharing.
